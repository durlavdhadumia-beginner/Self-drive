Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand plans can change. This policy explains how cancellations and refunds are handled on CarRentalNTravel.

Cancellation timeline

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours of pickup attract a charge equal to one day’s rental or the booking amount, whichever is lower.
  • No-shows without notice are billed at 100% of the booking amount.

Refund process

  • Approved refunds are initiated within 48 business hours of confirmation from our support team.
  • Depending on your payment method, the amount may take 5–7 business days to reflect in your bank account.
  • Security deposits (if any) are released after vehicle inspection and adjustment of dues.

Host-initiated cancellations

If a host cancels a confirmed booking, renters receive a full refund along with a 10% credit voucher for their next trip. We may also assess host penalties or temporarily suspend listings.

Need help?

For assistance with cancellations or refunds, reach us at support@carrentalntravel.com or call +91 98765 43210.
