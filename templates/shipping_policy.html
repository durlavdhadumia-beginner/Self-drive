Vehicle Delivery & Handover Policy

CarRentalNTravel is a self-drive car rental platform. We do not ship physical goods. Every booking includes a vehicle handover or pickup experience arranged between the renter and the host.

Pickup and drop-off

  • The default pickup point is the address shown on the search map and confirmed in your booking summary.
  • Hosts may offer door-step delivery within city limits at an additional charge. Delivery availability is shown before you complete the payment.
  • Exact pickup/drop timings must be respected. Late arrival beyond the allowed grace period can result in rescheduling fees.
